The TrendFind Journey

How a simple idea evolved into the most advanced product research platform

2018

The Spark of Inspiration

As a young innovator fascinated by both AI and ecommerce, our founder Kichko recognized the challenges sellers faced in identifying profitable products. The initial concept of TrendFind was born from combining these two passions.

2019

First Prototype

After months of self-teaching coding and machine learning, the first functional prototype was developed. It analyzed basic Amazon sales data to identify potential winning products.

2020

Beta Launch

The beta version launched with 50 early adopters. Their feedback helped refine the algorithms and user experience, proving the concept's viability in the market.

2021

Platform Expansion

TrendFind expanded beyond Amazon to include Walmart, AliExpress, and eBay data. The team grew to include data scientists and ecommerce experts.

2022

AI Breakthrough

Our proprietary AI model achieved 92% accuracy in predicting trending products 6-8 weeks before they peaked, giving our users a significant competitive advantage.

2023

Enterprise Launch

We introduced enterprise solutions for larger ecommerce businesses and agencies, serving over 15,000 active sellers worldwide.

2024

Global Recognition

TrendFind was featured in Forbes, Entrepreneur, and several ecommerce publications as the leading AI product research tool in the industry.

2025

The Future

With advanced predictive analytics and global market expansion, we're pioneering the next generation of ecommerce intelligence tools.
