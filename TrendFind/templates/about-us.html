About TrendFind

Our Mission

To arm every reseller, dropshipper and e-commerce entrepreneur with data-driven clarity, so they can spot tomorrow’s winning products today — faster, smarter and with confidence.

Why We Exist

In a marketplace moving at break-neck speed, guessing is expensive. TrendFind sifts through real-time retail feeds, historical sell-through and social-buzz signals to remove the guesswork and surface true demand before it peaks.

How We Help

Our AI ranks products by profitability, competition and seasonality; tracks price swings across 10+ marketplaces; and alerts you to breakout trends 24/7. You spend less time scraping data and more time scaling sales.

The Future of TrendFind

Next on our roadmap: live supplier stock APIs, social-influencer tie-ins, and predictive demand curves powered by federated learning. Our vision is to become the command-centre for every data-driven seller on the planet.
