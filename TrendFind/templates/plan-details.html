Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll only pay the prorated difference for the current billing cycle.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with just a few clicks, no questions asked.

How does the free trial work?

Our 7-day free trial gives you full access to all Pro plan features. No credit card is required to start the trial. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, your chosen plan will automatically begin.

What if I need more than 100 daily searches?

Our Enterprise plan offers unlimited searches, perfect for high-volume users. If you're on the Pro plan and occasionally need more searches, you can purchase additional search packs or upgrade temporarily.
