Getting Started

How do I create a TrendFind account?

Creating your TrendFind account is a seamless process designed to get you started quickly:

  1. Visit our signup page and choose your preferred authentication method (email or social login)
  2. Complete your profile by providing basic business information to personalize your experience
  3. Select your plan - start with a free trial or choose a subscription that fits your needs
  4. Connect your ecommerce platforms (optional but recommended for full functionality)
  5. Set up your product preferences to receive tailored recommendations

Our onboarding wizard will guide you through each step, and you can always modify these settings later.

What information do I need to provide during setup?

To optimize your TrendFind experience, we recommend providing:

  • Business profile: Your ecommerce niche, target markets, and business size
  • Platform connections: Links to your Shopify, Amazon, or other storefronts
  • Product preferences: Categories you're interested in and any specific product requirements
  • Goals: Whether you're focused on product research, competitor analysis, or market expansion

This information helps our AI deliver more accurate and relevant product recommendations.

Is there a mobile app available?

While we currently offer a fully responsive web application, our native mobile apps are in development with these planned features:

  • iOS & Android apps with full platform functionality
  • Real-time push notifications for trending products
  • Mobile-optimized analytics dashboards
  • Offline access to saved product data

In the meantime, you can add TrendFind to your home screen for app-like access:

  1. Open TrendFind in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
  2. Tap the share button
  3. Select "Add to Home Screen"

Core Features

How does TrendFind identify winning products?

Our proprietary AI analyzes multiple data dimensions to surface high-potential products:

  • Sales Velocity Tracking: Real-time monitoring of sell-through rates across major platforms
  • Social Sentiment Analysis: AI-powered evaluation of engagement across TikTok, Instagram, and Twitter
  • Search Trend Correlation: Cross-referencing with Google Trends and other search data
  • Competitor Benchmarking: Identifying what successful sellers in your niche are offering
  • Seasonal Pattern Recognition: Predictive modeling based on historical seasonal trends

Each product receives a TrendScore™ that quantifies its market potential based on these factors.

What retailers and marketplaces do you track?

TrendFind monitors over 75 global retail platforms including:

  • Amazon (all global marketplaces)
  • eBay
  • Walmart Marketplace
  • Etsy
  • Wayfair
  • AliExpress
  • Target Plus
  • Best Buy Marketplace
  • Home Depot
  • + 65 niche platforms

Our data partnerships ensure comprehensive coverage across all major ecommerce channels.

How accurate are your profit margin forecasts?

Our margin prediction engine achieves 88-92% accuracy when all cost factors are provided, analyzing:

  • Product Costs: Supplier pricing, MOQs, and bulk discounts
  • Logistics: Shipping fees, customs duties, and fulfillment costs
  • Marketplace Fees: Platform commissions, FBA fees, and payment processing
  • Advertising Costs: Historical CPC data for similar products
  • Competitive Pricing: Market positioning and price elasticity

For optimal accuracy, connect your supplier accounts and regularly update cost information.

Can I track multiple international markets?

Our Pro and Enterprise plans support multi-market analysis with these capabilities:

  • Market Comparison Dashboard: Visualize product performance across regions
  • Localized Trend Detection: Identify products trending in one market but not others
  • Regional Pricing Intelligence: Understand optimal pricing for each locale
  • Compliance Monitoring: Track product restrictions and regulations by country

This feature is particularly valuable for sellers expanding globally or testing new markets.

Billing & Accounts

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment options globally:

  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Bank Transfers: Available for annual Enterprise subscriptions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

You can modify your subscription at any time with these options:

  • Immediate Upgrades: Gain instant access to higher-tier features with prorated billing
  • End-of-Cycle Downgrades: Changes apply at your next billing date
  • Temporary Boosts: Purchase additional search packs or feature access as needed

To change your plan:

  1. Navigate to Account Settings > Billing
  2. Select "Change Plan"
  3. Choose your preferred subscription level
  4. Confirm the changes
How does the free trial work?

Our 7-day Pro trial offers full platform access with these benefits:

  • No payment method required to begin
  • Unlimited product searches during the trial period
  • Complete feature access including AI recommendations and analytics
  • Reminder notifications before trial expiration

If you don't cancel, your account automatically continues with the Pro plan at $29/month. You can cancel anytime during the trial with no obligation.

Technical Support

What are the system requirements?

TrendFind is optimized for these environments:

  • Browsers: Chrome (latest), Firefox (latest), Safari (latest), Edge (Chromium)
  • Operating Systems: Windows 10+, macOS 10.15+, Linux (Chromium-based browsers)
  • Display: Minimum 1280×720 resolution recommended
  • Internet: 10Mbps+ connection for optimal performance

For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome on a desktop with at least 8GB RAM.

How often is your data updated?

Our data refresh cycles vary by source:

Data Type Update Frequency
Marketplace Sales Data Every 1-2 hours
Social Media Trends Real-time (15 min delay)
Search Volume Data Daily
Supplier Pricing On refresh or change notification
Competitor Analysis Every 4 hours

Enterprise users can request custom update schedules for critical data.

How do I integrate with Shopify/WooCommerce?

Connecting your ecommerce platform takes just minutes:

  1. Navigate to Integrations in your TrendFind account
  2. Select your platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.)
  3. Authenticate using your store admin credentials
  4. Configure data sharing preferences
  5. Sync your product catalog and sales data

Once connected, you'll gain access to platform-specific insights like:

  • Shopify product performance benchmarks
  • WooCommerce conversion rate comparisons
  • Platform-specific trend alerts

Advanced Features

How does the AI Recommendation Engine work?

Our proprietary recommendation system uses multiple AI models:

  • Collaborative Filtering: Analyzes successful products among similar sellers
  • Content-Based Filtering: Matches products to your historical preferences
  • Hybrid Model: Combines both approaches with market trend data
  • Deep Learning: Continuously improves suggestions based on your feedback

The system weights these factors to generate personalized product recommendations with confidence scores.

Can I use the API for custom integrations?

  • Custom Alerts: Create personalized notification triggers
  • Workflow Automation: Integrate with your inventory management tools
  • White-Label Dashboards: Embed TrendFind analytics in your internal tools

API documentation is available to Enterprise customers through our developer portal.
