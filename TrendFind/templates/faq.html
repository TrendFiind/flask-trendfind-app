Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TrendFind and how it can help you discover winning products for your ecommerce business.

Getting Started

How do I create a TrendFind account?

Creating a TrendFind account is quick and easy:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Verify your email address by clicking the link we send you
  4. Complete your profile by adding basic information about your business
  5. Choose your preferred plan (you can start with a free trial)

Once completed, you'll have immediate access to TrendFind's powerful product research tools.

Is there a free version of TrendFind?

Yes! TrendFind offers a free Starter plan that includes:

  • 10 daily product searches
  • Basic trending data and analytics
  • Access to limited retailers
  • Ability to save up to 5 products

This plan is perfect for those who want to test our platform before committing to a paid subscription.

What information do I need to provide to get started?

To get the most out of TrendFind, we recommend providing:

  • Your primary ecommerce platform (Shopify, Amazon, eBay, etc.)
  • Your target market/country
  • Your product niche or categories of interest
  • Your business size (small, medium, or large)

This information helps us personalize your experience and provide more relevant product recommendations.

Features & Functionality

How does TrendFind identify trending products?

TrendFind uses a sophisticated AI algorithm that analyzes multiple data points to identify trending products:

  • Sales velocity: Tracks how quickly products are selling across major platforms
  • Social media buzz: Monitors mentions and engagement on platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and Twitter
  • Search trends: Analyzes search volume patterns on Google and other search engines
  • Competitor analysis: Identifies what successful sellers in your niche are offering
  • Seasonal patterns: Predicts upcoming trends based on historical seasonal data

Our system weights these factors to give each product a "trend score" that helps you identify the best opportunities.

What retailers does TrendFind track?

TrendFind currently tracks products from over 50 major retailers including:

  • Amazon (all marketplaces)
  • eBay
  • Walmart
  • AliExpress
  • Etsy
  • Wayfair
  • Target
  • Best Buy
  • Home Depot
  • And many more niche marketplaces

We're constantly adding new retailers based on user demand. If there's a specific retailer you'd like us to add, please contact our support team.

How accurate are the profit margin forecasts?

Our profit margin forecasts are typically within 10-15% of actual margins when all costs are properly accounted for. The forecasts consider:

  • Product cost from suppliers
  • Shipping fees (both to you and to customers)
  • Marketplace fees (Amazon FBA, eBay, etc.)
  • Payment processing fees
  • Average advertising costs for similar products
  • Competitive pricing in your target market

For the most accurate forecasts, we recommend connecting your supplier accounts and updating the cost information regularly.

Can I track products in multiple countries?

Yes! With our Pro and Enterprise plans, you can track product trends in multiple countries simultaneously. This feature allows you to:

  • Compare product popularity across different markets
  • Identify products that are trending in one country but not yet popular in others
  • Get localized pricing and competition data
  • View country-specific shipping and tax implications

This is particularly valuable for sellers looking to expand internationally or test new markets.

Billing & Plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfers (for annual Enterprise plans)

All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment partners. We don't store your full payment details on our servers.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Click on "Billing"
  3. Select "Change Plan"
  4. Choose your new plan

When upgrading, you'll immediately gain access to the new features and be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, changes take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No, all TrendFind plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with just a few clicks.

We do offer discounts for annual subscriptions (save up to 20%), but even these can be canceled at any time with a prorated refund for unused months.

How does the free trial work?

Our 7-day free trial gives you full access to all Pro plan features:

  • No credit card required to start the trial
  • Full access to all features for 7 days
  • Unlimited product searches during trial period
  • Access to all retailer data and analytics

If you don't cancel before the trial ends, your account will automatically continue with the Pro plan at $29/month. You'll receive email reminders before your trial ends.

Technical Questions

What browsers are supported?

TrendFind works best on the latest versions of:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

We recommend using Chrome for the best performance. Some features may not work properly on older browser versions or mobile browsers.

Do you have a mobile app?

Currently, TrendFind is a web-based platform that works on mobile browsers, but we don't have dedicated iOS or Android apps yet.

However, you can add TrendFind to your home screen:

  1. Open TrendFind in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
  2. Tap the share button
  3. Select "Add to Home Screen"

This will create an app-like icon that launches TrendFind in a standalone window. We're working on native mobile apps that will be released later this year.

How often is the data updated?

Our data updates at different frequencies depending on the information type:

  • Sales data: Updated every 2-4 hours for most retailers
  • Social media trends: Updated in real-time
  • Search trends: Updated daily
  • Supplier pricing: Updated when you refresh or when suppliers notify us of changes

Enterprise plan users can request more frequent updates for specific products or retailers.

How do I integrate TrendFind with my Shopify store?

Connecting your Shopify store takes just a few minutes:

  1. Go to "Integrations" in your TrendFind account
  2. Click "Connect Shopify"
  3. Enter your Shopify store URL
  4. Authenticate with Shopify's secure login
  5. Select the data you want to share (we recommend all options for best results)

Once connected, TrendFind will analyze your store's performance and provide personalized product recommendations based on your existing catalog and sales data.
