Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI-powered product research platform

TrendFind is an AI-powered product research platform designed specifically for ecommerce sellers, dropshippers, and resellers. Our system works by:

  • Scanning multiple marketplaces (Amazon, Walmart, AliExpress, eBay) in real-time
  • Analyzing social media trends to identify emerging product opportunities
  • Processing millions of data points including reviews, ratings, pricing history, and competition levels
  • Using machine learning algorithms to predict which products are likely to trend before they become oversaturated
  • Providing actionable insights with profit calculations, supplier comparisons, and market analysis

TrendFind stands out through several key differentiators:

  • Predictive AI Technology: We don't just show you what's already trending - we predict what will trend next based on early signals
  • Multi-Source Data Integration: We combine marketplace data with social media trends and historical sales patterns
  • Profit-First Approach: Every product recommendation includes detailed profit margin calculations including all fees
  • Global Market Insights: Our system tracks regional availability and shipping options for international sellers
  • Continuous Learning: Our AI improves its recommendations based on your saved products and success metrics

Our AI analyzes a comprehensive set of data points to identify high-potential products:

  • Marketplace Data: Sales rank, price history, review velocity, rating distribution, seller count
  • Social Signals: Mentions on TikTok, Instagram, Twitter; engagement rates, sentiment analysis
  • Competitive Landscape: Number of similar products, review quality comparison, pricing strategies
  • Logistics Factors: Shipping times, supplier locations, fulfillment options
  • Seasonal Trends: Historical sales patterns, upcoming events, cultural trends
  • Economic Indicators: Consumer spending patterns, niche market growth rates

Our profit margin calculations are 95%+ accurate when all relevant fees are accounted for. The system includes:

  • Marketplace fees: Commission percentages, referral fees, closing fees
  • Payment processing: Stripe, PayPal, or other gateway fees
  • Shipping costs: Both domestic and international rates
  • Product costs: Including bulk purchase discounts when available
  • Advertising estimates: Average CPC for similar products
  • Returns buffer: Typical return rates for the product category

You can customize these parameters in your account settings for even more precise calculations.
